Gujarati Lesson Plan

Day 1

Good morning - Suprabhat (soo-pra-baat)

Thank you - Aabhari (aa-bha-ree)

Yes - Ha (huh)

No - Na (nuh)

Excuse me - Maaf karo (maaf kaa-ro)

Day 2

Hello - Kem chho (kem-cho)

How are you? - Kem cho? (kem-cho)

I'm fine - Mane bhalo lagyo (maa-nay baa-lo luh-gyo)

What is your name? - Tamaru naam su che? (tuh-maa-ru naam soo chay)

My name is... - Maa-rum naam... che (maa-ru naam... chay)

Day 3

Goodbye - Aavjo (aa-vjo)

See you soon - Jaldi milo (juhl-dee mee-lo)

How much does it cost? - Kati paise? (kuh-tee pie-say)

I don't understand - Mane samajh nathi aave (maa-nay suh-muh-jh nuh-thee aa-vay)

Please speak slowly - Dayra vaagar boljo (dye-ruh vaa-gar bol-jo)

Day 30

Congratulations! - Badhaai ho! (bud-haa-ee ho)

You did a great job! - Tame khubj saru karyu! (tuh-may khub-juh suh-roo kuh-ryu)

Thank you for your help - Tamaru sahaay che (tuh-maa-ru sah-haa-y chay)

Can you help me? - Mane sahaay karso? (maa-nay sah-haa-y kar-so)

Good luck! - Shubhechha! (shub-huh-chha)
