Welcome to the College Photography Competition

Enter your best photographs to win amazing prizes and get recognized by the jury members.

The competition has several categories, including landscape, portrait, wildlife, and abstract. The entry fee is $10 per photograph.

The jury members are experts in the field of photography and will select the winners based on creativity, technical skills, and originality.

The top three winners in each category will receive a cash prize of $1000, $500, and $250 respectively. All winners will also get a feature in our magazine and an invitation to the award ceremony.

The deadline for submissions is June 1, 2023. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your talent and win amazing prizes!
