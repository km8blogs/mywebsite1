About Us

Artifacts was founded by a group of college graduates who wanted to find a unique way to celebrate and remember their college experiences. They noticed that there were few options available for alumni to display their college memories in a way that was both personalized and elegant. Artifacts was born out of the idea of creating high-quality custom art pieces that tell the stories of students' college experiences.

How We Started

The idea for Artifacts was first conceived when the founders were looking for a special graduation gift for a close friend. They wanted to give something that was both unique and meaningful, but they couldn't find anything that fit the bill. They decided to create a custom art piece that featured their friend's favorite places on campus, and the idea for Artifacts was born. The founders realized that there was a market for custom art pieces that captured the essence of college memories, and they decided to turn their idea into a business.

FAQ

  • What kind of photos can I use?

    You can use any photos you have taken yourself, or that you have the rights to use. We can also provide you with a selection of high-quality stock images to choose from.

  • What is the turnaround time for custom orders?

    Custom orders typically take 2-3 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity of the design and the availability of materials.

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we will work with you to make it right or offer a full refund.
